 
Actors:
  a. User: Public user that is not necessarily authenticated looking for information about research projects. All authenticated actors would also have the goals for the User actor in addition to their own goals.
  b. Requester: Authenticated user requesting to conduct a new research project
  c. Site manager: Authenticated user that facilitates completion of information needed to evaluate the impacts and feasibility of proposed research projects. Serves as a point of contact between the requester and the site leadership.
  d. Site leadership:  Authenticated user(s) responsible for approving/disapproving new research project requests for a site.
  e. Writer: Authenticated user(s) collecting information and either supplying content for or writing the annual report for a site
  f. Administrator: Authenticated user(s) responsible for maintenance of all research project information for a site.
  g. Responsible investigator: Person responsible for a research project(s) that maintains their own research project(s) information or the responsible investigators 
delegate. 

  h. Research assistant: Authenticated user(s) responsible for setting up research sites, collecting and quality checking data, analyzing data, and/or maintaining research equipment and sites. (Field or lab technicians)
